The Most Popular Social Media Sites of 2020

(MAUs stands for Monthly Active Users, and MUVs stands for Monthly Unique Visitors.)

  1. Facebook – 2.23 billion MAUs
  2. Instagram – 1 billion MAUs
  3. YouTube – 1.9 billion MAUs
  4. SnapChat – 255 million MAUs
  5. Linked in – 294 million MAUs
  6. Pinterest – 250 million MAUs
  7. WhatsApp – 1.5 billion MAUs
  8. Twitter – 335 million MAUs
